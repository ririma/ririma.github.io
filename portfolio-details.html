Predicting Loan Status Based on Key Banking Factors

Project Summary

Analyzing a loan dataset containing historical data of bank customers, focusing on distinguishing between those who tend to default on their loans and those who do not. Identifying high-risk customers for potential loan defaults is a crucial approach to minimize lender losses. Thus, we will attempt to predict the likelihood of customer loan defaults using provided predictors. The target column is labeled 'Status,' with values 0 indicating non-default and 1 indicating default.
