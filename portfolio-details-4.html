Overview

BPJPH stands for "Badan Penyelenggara Jaminan Produk Halal," which translates to the "Halal Product Assurance Organizing Agency" in English. This agency is responsible for overseeing and managing the certification of halal products in Indonesia. It ensures that products conform to Islamic dietary laws and regulations, making them permissible for consumption by Muslims. The data used is a halal certification dataset from the years 2020 to 2023.
