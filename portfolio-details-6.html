Overview

The Halal Inspection Institute (LPH) is an institution established by the government and society through an Islamic religious institution, which is a legal entity. LPH appoints halal auditors who are tasked with conducting inspections, studies, and research to guarantee the halalness of food, medicine, and beverage products produced both on a small scale (MSMEs) and by large companies. This dashboard uses Excel Pivot tables. The LPH dashboard contains data on the distribution of LPH scope, LPH status, distribution areas, etc.
